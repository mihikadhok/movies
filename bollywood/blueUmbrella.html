The Blue Umbrella

Description

When a few Japanese tourists gift Biniya a blue umbrella, she gains immense popularity. However, Nandakishore, the only shopkeeper in their village, tries to get his hands on her umbrella.

My Personal Opinion

This movie is really sweet. It is a Hindi movie. You can even show it to Aai Baba, they love these kinds of movies. You might find it a bit slow in places, but overall, it's nice. BTW, it is based on a novel with the same name as the movie, written by Ruskin Bond.
